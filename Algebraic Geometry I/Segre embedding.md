## $\mathbb{P}^1_k \times_k \mathbb{P}^1_k \to \mathbb{P}^3_k$

Posem $\mathbb{P}^1_k = Proj(k[X_0,X_1])$, $\mathbb{P}^1_k = Proj(k[Y_0,Y_1])$ les dues c√≤pies. Aleshores$$\mathbb{P}^1_k \times_k \mathbb{P}^1_k \cong Proj(k[X_0,X_1] \bar\otimes_k k[Y_0,Y_1]),$$on $\bar\otimes_k$ denota el producte tensorial grau-a-grau. Denotem $\mathcal{O} = \mathcal{O}_{\mathbb{P}^1_k \times_k \mathbb{P}^1_k}$.

Les seccions globals de $\mathcal{O}(1)$ estan generades (com a $k$-espai vectorial) per $X_0 \otimes Y_0, X_0 \otimes Y_1, X_1 \otimes Y_0, X_1 \otimes Y_1$.
